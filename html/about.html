Beyond Sight: Independence with Technology

The Beginning

When a friend asked me to share weekly Apple tips, I started creating 20-30 minute videos and sharing them through Google Drive in 2018.

AppleTalk

In September 2020, I launched my YouTube channel under the name AppleTalk and began to develop my content creation skills.

iAccessAbility

As the channel evolved, so did my vision for it. In early 2024, I rebranded the channel to iAccessAbility, focusing on the accessibility side of technology. I began developing the website and bought the domain in 2025 and continue to add to and improve the site. I share my knowledge of accessibility features and productivity tips through my YouTube channel and support articles.

Future Plans

I hope to eventually vlog about my life, providing personal insight into my experiences as a visually impaired albino.

Beyond Sight: My Story

Now you're probably wondering, “Who runs iAccessAbility?” Hi, I'm Leah! I've been a lifelong Apple fan and collector, passionate accessibility advocate, and the heart behind the camera and the site. For over 8 years, I've relied on Apple products to navigate life, create freely, and connect with the world. Now, I strive to help others experience the amazing opportunities technology can offer us all.
