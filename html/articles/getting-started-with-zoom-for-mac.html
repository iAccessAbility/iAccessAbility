Getting Started with the Accessibility Zoom Feature for Mac

The Zoom feature on Mac allows a user to magnify anything on the display at any time in any application. You can enlarge the display entirely or in a resizable window. This article will go over how to setup, enable, use, and disable Zoom.


Locate and Setup

  1. Open Settings
  2. Clcik Accessibility
  3. Click Zoom
  4. Toggle on Use Keyboard Shortcuts to Zoom
  5. (Optional) Toggle on USe Trackpad Gestures to Zoom

Use Zoom

  • Double-tap the screen with three figners to zoom in and out
  • Double-tap and swipe up and down with three fingers to adjust the magnification or triple-tap with three fingers to bring up the quick settings, then slide the slider to adjust the magnification.
  • Drag with three fingers around the screen to move the magnification window around.
