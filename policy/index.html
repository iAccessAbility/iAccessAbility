Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2025

Thank you for visiting the iAccessAbility Services website. We wish to respect your privacy and be sure you understand what we do with personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information.

Information Collected

No personal information is collected and recorded unless you reach out to us directly via email, or interaction on our social media profiles. Anything with an * is required when contacting us for services.

  • Your name* (First name (full), Last initial at minimum)
  • Your preferred name* (how you would like us to recognize you as publicly on our reviews page)
  • Your phone number*
  • Your email address*
  • Your device details (model, software version, and potentially personal information you voluntarily share)
  • Any other information you include in your message

How We Use Information

Any information you provide is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries or messages personally
  • Improve our services and content
  • Properly quote you if and when you submit any public reviews.

How We Store Information

Your name and contact information are stored securely in Google Contacts under the iAccessAbility account and are kept until you request deletion. Emails exchanged with iAccessAbility are managed through Zoho Mail. Emails sent to
• support@iaccessabilityservices.com
• request@iaccessabilityservices.com
• policy@iaccessabilityservices.com
are automatically deleted after one year. Emails sent to
• feedback@iaccessabilityservices.com
• report@iaccessabilityservices.com
• reviews@iaccessabilityservices.com
are kept indefinitely until deletion is requested. Emails sent to
• business@iaccessabilityservices.com
are deleted when we no longer need them and do not have a definitive deletion time period.

We do not sell or freely share your personal information with third parties.

External Links

Our website contains links to external websites such as Apple Support, Google Support, and other support resources and articles as well as various third party apps and services. iAccessAbility is not responsible for the privacy practices of referenced companies, their products, or services. We strongly encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.
